Chocolate Hummus

No. Just No.

Here's a recipe for normal hummus. You're welcome.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of chickpeas
  • 2 tbs tahini
  • juice of 1/2 a lemon
  • 2 to 4 cloves of garlic
  • 3 tbs of olive oil (plus more for garnish)
  • a sprinkle of paprika or sumac for topping

Steps

  1. Rinse the chickpeas and put all of the ingredients into a food processor or blender.
  2. DO NOT ADD CHOCOLATE IN ANY SHAPE OR FORM!
  3. Plate with a drizzle of oilve oil and topping. Enjoy!
